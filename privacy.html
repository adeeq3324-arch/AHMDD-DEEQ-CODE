Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Welcome to Phone Finder. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data: Since this app is a Phone Finder, we request permission to access your device's real-time GPS location (Latitude and Longitude) to display it on the map and help you find your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your location data is processed locally on your device and transmitted strictly to show your device's position on the map. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal location data with third-party companies.

3. Data Security

We implement security measures to ensure your data is secure. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through our official project page.
